Mayank Sharan

Applied Scientist II, Microsoft Turing
Redmond, WA, USA

I am an Applied Scientist II in the Microsoft Turing team. I work at the cutting edge of applied AI with large language models, computer vision and much more.

Research: I have broad interests in machine learning and artificial intelligence. Currently, my work focuses on natural language processing, spatiotemporal machine learning, and reinforcement learning. In the past, I have worked with recommender systems, causal inference, and computer vision.

Previously: I completed my Masters with Thesis in Computer Science and Engineering at UCSD under Prof. Rose Yu. Before that, I worked as a Senior Data Scientist at Bidgely. I obtained my Bachelor’s in Computer Science and Engineering, from Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi where I worked with Prof. Mausam and Prof. Rahul Garg.
